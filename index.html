Helpful tips for getting the most from this course

Here are some helpful tips for getting the most from this course. You will be scrolling through the content and clicking to advance to the next section. You can also use the course navigation to move from section to section. You can revisit the content as often as you wish. Helpful resources and tools are located behind the Tools in the header.

As Dr. Gerber referenced, you will be presented with reflective questions throughout this course. In some instances, you will be selecting an answer and will then how your choice compares with others who have answered that question. More in-depth feedback from Dr. Gerber may also be provided.

In other instances, you will be entering your response and will then be able to see how others answered, providing valuable differing perspectives. As you progress throughout the course, you will be working towards developing your own action plan, which will be available to you to print upon completion of the course. Certain key points and quotes will be called out for you as important, and there will be additional printable resources available to you.

You can reaccess this course as often as you wish and we hope you have found it helpful!
